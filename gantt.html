Handwritten notes are OK on accountability assignment

FYI on the reading accountability assignment it is 100% ok for you to take handwritten notes and attached a photo of them for accountability. They don't need to be well presented/formatted or have nice handwriting - the goal is that they are useful to you, more than anything. I personally get a lot more out of reading/lecture notes when I am writing them out.
